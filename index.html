About Me

I am a multi-skilled Engineer passionate about renewable energy and leadership. I have designed over 100 solar systems for organizations across Zambia during the country’s worst energy crisis.

I have served as Committee Member, Finance Secretary, and Vice President for the Engineering Institution of Zambia – Mulungushi University Chapter. I also co-founded the Mulungushi University Robotics Society and contributed as a member of the Students’ Representative Council.

Currently, I work with Lunsemfwa Hydro Power Company in operations and maintenance and volunteer with Engineers Without Borders to bring energy access to rural communities.
